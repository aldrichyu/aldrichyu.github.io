Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK). I also work closely with Dr. Rongyao Fang. I anticipate completing my doctorate in 2027. Before that, I studied at Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

My research direction is Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), with a focus on visual understanding and generation. I am committed to developing integrated systems that can perceive, understand, and generate visual content by leveraging advanced techniques from multimodal large language models.
